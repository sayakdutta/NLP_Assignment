As a frequent traveler in the Houston area, Continental Airlines would
like to tell you about a new guaranteed parking service at Bush
Intercontinental Airport (IAH) being introduced by the City of Houston
Aviation Department.

The IAH Sure Park is a members-only service that guarantees you a parking
space on a secure floor (2nd level) via a private entrance in the C East
Garage, along with a private expedited exit.  The C East Garage is
adjacent to Terminal C and parking will be available 24 hours a day, 365
days a year.   There is a one-time application fee of $200 along with a
$50 refundable deposit for the access card.  When you use the IAH SurePark
facility, the charge is $15 per 24 hour period.  For more information or
to obtain a membership application, please call the Houston Airport
Management office at 281-230-3006 or e-mail your request to


IAH Sure Park is a service managed in whole by the City of Houston
Aviation Department and is offered on a first come, first served basis.
