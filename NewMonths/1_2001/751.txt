ENERGY DERIVATIVES 
PRICING AND RISK MANAGEMENT 
Les Clewlow and Chris Strickland
 This book provides a comprehensive and technical treatment of the 
 valuation and risk management of energy derivatives, including: 
the behaviour of spot and forward prices 
modelling and estimating volatility 
analysis of structures that exist in the energy derivatives market and their 
applications 
the pricing and hedging of energy derivatives using single and multi-factor 
spot price models 
using simulation and trinomial trees for pricing caps, floors, swaptions, and 
path-dependent securities such as barriers, Asians, lookbacks, and swing 
options 
using multi-factor models of the forward curve, including volatility 
estimation and pricing of exotic derivatives 
risk management of energy derivative positions 
value-at-risk and credit risk for energy derivatives
