 Illinova is the holding company for Illinois Power and Illinova Generating.
 Illinois Power contributes more than 70% of Illinovas sales -- generating
 and selling electric energy and transporting and selling natural gas. Coal
 fuels most of its electricity generation. Illinova Generating invests in
 energy projects worldwide. Illinova Energy Partners, an Illinova subsidiary,
 brokers, markets, and sells gas, electric power, and energy-related
 services. Another subsidiary provides insurance for Illinova and its other
 businesses. Illinois Power and eight other firms that own power transmission
 facilities are forming an independent system operator group to prepare for
 wholesale power transmission by deregulated utilities.
