>
> The New York Mercantile Exchange, Inc., today announced that it will
close
> on Friday, November 23, and hold reduced trading hours through the end of
> 2001:
>
> The board made these decisions to provide some relief in consideration of
> continued transportation difficulties and the overwhelming desire
> expressed to us by our customers and traders to spend the holiday with
> their families. After the events of last month, enabling families to be
> together has become more critical than ever.
>
> All contract expiration dates will remain unchanged, including the light,
> sweet crude oil options expiration scheduled for Wednesday, November 14,
> and the crude oil futures expiration scheduled for Monday, November 19.
>
> The COMEX Division futures and options contracts will begin opening
> earlier on November 5, with the rest of the opening times and all closing
> times remaining the same.
>
