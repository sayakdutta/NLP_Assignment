
 
 In Europe this week FOEX showed an strong increase in both softwoods and hardwoods. This is the second increase for NBSK this year and it is a sign of successful efforts on the producers side to raise prices.
 NBSK was $458.89 up $7.76 (1.72%)
 BHKP was Euro 452.98 up Euro 9.25 (or 2%). Expressed in dollars BHKP came in at $415.48 up from $407.11.
