 SUBJECT:
 END OF MIDDLE EAST SOUR CRUDE OIL FUTURES CONTRACT
 
 
 The NYMEX Board of Directors decided today, Wednesday, April 11, 2001, to 
 cease trading of the Middle East Sour Crude oil futures contract effective 
 immediately.  The contract was available only on the NYMEX ACCESSc system.
 
 The Middle East Sour Crude oil futures settlement index will no longer be 
 published as a result of this action by the Board of Directors.
 
 If you have any questions, please contact Jeffrey Kerr at (212) 299-2615.
