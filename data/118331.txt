 The Index for a month shall be the mean of the arithmetic averages of the 
 prices per Short Ton for each such calendar month for Short Ton of 26 Lb. 
 Semichemical Medium, delivered in the Eastern U.S. as listed under the 
 heading: "Price Watch: Paperboard/Packaging: Grade: Corrugated medium (26 
 lb): Semichemical, East" in the issue of Pulp and Paper Week that reports 
 prices effective for the calendar month.
