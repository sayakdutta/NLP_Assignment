  The Edison Electric Institute presents a timely and topical workshop:
 *Power Marketing Transactions Using Master Contracts to Mitigate Risk* at the 
 Four Seasons Hotel, Newport Beach, California on April 18-19, 2001.
 Join the developers of the EEI Master Contract and the Western Systems Power 
 Pool Agreement in a one-day workshop devoted to managing power marketing 
 risks using these agreements.
 We promise the lights will be on!
 Program Highlights:
 Becky A. Kilbourne, formerly with the California Power Exchange offers her 
 observations on the *California Meltdown* and presents a new paradigm for 
 electricity markets.
 Marvin J. Goldstein, Partner, Stroock, Stroock & Lavan, explains how to 
 protect yourself in the event of a counter-party bankruptcy.
 Join us for a Welcoming Reception at 6:30 pm on the evening of April 18th and 
 after the Workshop on the 19th , join members of EEI*s Legal Committee at 
 6:30 pm for the Welcoming Reception for the Spring 2001 Legal Committee 
 Conference.
